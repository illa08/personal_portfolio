My Tech Journey

My journey began in middle school when the whole class went to the computer lab for the first time and we were told that we can create our own website just by typing '!' and pressing enter, we did exactly what we were told not having a single knowledge about the words that appeared and everyone wrote an essay about themselves but it was like magic for us that we could do such a thing and hence began my journey for curiosity.

I started learning Python as my first programming language in my high school and printed my very first 'Hello World'. The initial learning phase was a bit struggling but I finally understood the basics and built a rock, paper and scissor game.
